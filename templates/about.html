About Go Logic Institute

Empowering minds through quality education and innovative learning approaches since 2016.

Our Story

Go Logic Institute was founded in December 2016 with a vision to transform education through technology and innovation. What began as a small coaching center with just 10 PCs has now evolved into a leading institute offering diverse courses in design, engineering, programming, data science, and more.

Over the years, we have helped thousands of students achieve their career goals through our comprehensive curriculum, experienced faculty, and industry-focused approach to learning.

At Go Logic, we believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone. That's why we've designed our courses to be affordable, flexible, and relevant to the current job market demands.

Our Journey

2016

Our Beginning

Established on Dec 13, 2016 with 10 PCs and 10 students.

Initial courses: AutoCAD, 3ds Max, CATIA, CorelDraw, Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop

2017

First Expansion

Expanded to 15 PCs to meet growing demand and serve more students.

2018

Growth & Innovation

Expanded to 20 PCs.

Introduced Google SketchUp with VRay.

Major syllabus revision to meet industry standards.

2019

Industry Recognition

Launched job assistance programs.

Local industries began recruiting directly from our institute.

2020

Pandemic Adaptation

Shifted to online classes during the pandemic.

Provided continuous guidance and support for student projects.

2021

Experiential Learning

Introduced real-world exposure through:

  • Machine & parts workshop visits
  • Construction site visits
  • Interior design project exploration
2022

Renewed Popularity

Gained reputation through word-of-mouth publicity.

Student enrollments increased steadily.

2023

Market Challenges

Faced competition from online platforms.

Students returned, valuing our interactive, supportive environment.

2024

Tech Expansion

Launched Programming & Coding Department in October 2024.

New courses: C/C++, Java, Python, MERN Stack, AI & ML, Java Full Stack, Data Science

2025

New Horizons

Inaugurated second branch on Feb 28, 2025 near SSVPS Engineering College.

Continuing to empower students with real-world skills.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, industry-relevant education that equips students with the skills, knowledge, and mindset needed to excel in today's competitive job market.

We strive to create a learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities while ensuring our students are prepared for the challenges of the digital age.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier institution for technical and professional education, known for producing skilled professionals who make a meaningful impact in their respective fields.

We aspire to build a global community of lifelong learners who continuously adapt to changing technologies and contribute to innovation and growth in the digital economy.
